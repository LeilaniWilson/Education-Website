This is a map of South America!

 

The largest river in South America is the Amazon River.

It is used for transportation.

It is also called the River Sea

Anaconda Snakes are found in the shallow waters of the Amazon River

 

An Animal unique to South America is the Coati.

Found in wet jungles and the forest

There are four types, but the ring tailed and the mountain tailed are the two found in South America

They are omnivores that eat many plants and small insects and small animals
